Civic activity

For the past 6 years I have been an active member of the Clio student corporations at Petro Mohyla Black Sea National University. Even after graduation, I continue to take an active part in the life of the student union and keep in touch with professors.

While studying at the university, I participated in many activities related to student life. First of all, I was an active participant in the "open day" at Petro Mohyla Black Sea National University, for which I received an award from the Dean of the Faculty of Political Science.

Since I was 18, I have been writing complaints and inquiries to local deputies about the quality of the city's public infrastructure. Five years later, I started working as an assistant-consultant for the deputy and I started receiving these complaints for further processing.

In 2021 I joined the Community Based Organization "MISTO 2050" which acts in the interests of the city of Mykolaiv. That same year, the organization nominated me as a candidate for the Mayor's Youth Council, where I was elected by the end of 2021.

This year, the Mayor's Youth Council began its annual preparations for participation in the competition of the youth capital of Ukraine. Together with the members of the Mayor's Youth Council, I developed a program and concept of youth projects for the development of our city. Unfortunately, due to the war, initiatives and projects were frozen.

This year, I joined the public organization "National Association of Youth Advisors". The organization includes representatives of youth movements from all over Ukraine. In September 2022, we started dealing with organizational issues within our association. Currently, we have several projects planned at both the municipal and all-Ukrainian levels.

Political activity

Since the 2019 presidential election, I have been actively defending my political position and, as a member of civil society, I have voted in every election. In the parliamentary elections of the same 2019, I tried myself as an election observer for the first time.

In 2020, during the regional elections in Ukraine, I was appointed a representative of the press from the newspaper "NASHA HROMADA". I monitored the polling station and prevented any falsifications.

Working as a deputy's assistant-consultant, I gained extensive experience in working with documents and parliamentary activities. Often visiting the city council and seeing the work of party groups and the mayor, I set a goal to take an active part in the politics of the city, and later possibly the whole country.

Unfortunately, working in the Mayor's Youth Council, due to the lack of a deputy's rank, we could not influence the Mayor's decision, but we could advise. Our advice was quite valuable and therefore it is not uncommon for projects related to the city's youth policy to be implemented jointly by us together with the deputies.
